where creativity and community meet.

At partnr, we believe that collaboration between creative individuals is what fuels social impact and positive change in the world. We love to see two, or more, people come together and create cool and inspiring work. We have dedicated ourselves to build a tool that better facilitates connections amongst people - whether these connections are in your same city, or across the globe.

We are actively working to build partnr beta where you can build your creative network and collaborate with whoever you want. Sign-up for our e-mail list to be the first to experience the partnr movement.
